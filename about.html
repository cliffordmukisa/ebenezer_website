Who We Are?

Bugema Ebenezer SACCO was started in 2000 and registered with the registrar of cooperatives on 15th January 2000 with registration number ML 7098. It is located in Busiika Town Council, 32Km Gayaza-Zirobwe Road, Bamunanika County, Luweero district.

What We Offer?

We offer sustainable and affordable financial and non-financial services to the economically unstable individuals that helps them to eradicate poverty among themselves

Our Vision & Mission

To be a leading financial institution in provision of affordable financial services in the community.

To promote a saving and investment culture in the community by providing sustainable financial services.
